Hi! I am Amouchi Thamazgha

Digital And Traditional Artist

What I do

Digital Art

My main subjects are usually portraits and character designs done using Clip Studio Paint. I mostly draw my own characters and occasionally I do fanart or reference studies.

Traditional Art

I create art using various traditional media that include but are not limited to: oil paint, watercolor paint, ink, and graphite. These are my favorite for still life studies and quick practice.

Fiber Art

It all started with crochet and expanded to knitting, embroidery, beading and knotting. Products vary from clothes to amigurumi to bracelets and earrings.
